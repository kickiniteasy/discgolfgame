How to Play

  • Hold SPACE or tap/click THROW to charge your throw
  • Release to throw the disc
  • Use the camera controls to look around
  • Click to change discs
  • Click to view scores
  • Try to get the disc in the basket in the fewest throws!

Controls

  • Show Hole - Center camera on the hole
  • Reset Camera - Reset camera to player view
  • Long press - Toggle auto-target mode
  • Mouse/Touch drag - Look around
  • Mouse wheel/Pinch - Zoom in/out

Multiplayer & Custom Courses

  • Click and go to Players tab to add local players
  • Each player can customize their name and color
  • Create custom courses using any SVG editor (like Inkscape)
  • Upload your course in Settings ‚Üí Courses tab
  • See the course creation guide for details
